Why Cheese Curds?


Living so close to Wisconsin can lead some (like ourselves) to create strong feelings about the delicious nuggets. Many notice the lack of curd in the cheese curd as often restaurants will use regular cheese instead of the classic curd form or serve it with something other than ranch.


What's a Cheese Curd?

Check out this video showing the cheese curd making process! Unbeknownst to many, curds are different than regular cheese, and when warm (or fresh) can squeak!
